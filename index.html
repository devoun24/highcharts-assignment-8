Collegiate Crime in Alachua County

By: Devoun Cetoute

Students at the University of Florida and Santa Fe College make up 25 percent of the population of Alachua County. While their actions affect business development and the local economy, it also affects the crime rate. With UF having the largest population of the two, the first chart will be looking at the most reported crimes on campus since 2015. The second chart shows all crimes reported on both campuses since 2016.
